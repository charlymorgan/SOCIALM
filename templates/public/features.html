Features

  • User Profiles

    Create your personalized profile with information about yourself, profile picture, and privacy settings. Customize your profile to showcase your interests and personality.

  • News Feed

    Stay updated with the latest posts from your friends and accounts you follow. The news feed displays a chronological list of posts, photos, and videos shared by your connections.

  • Content Sharing

    Share your thoughts, photos, videos, and links with your network. Express yourself through text posts or enhance your content with multimedia elements.

  • Interactions

    Engage with other users by liking, commenting on, and sharing their posts. Start conversations, express your support, or share interesting content with your connections.

  • Direct Messaging

    Communicate privately with your friends and connections through direct messaging. Share messages, photos, and videos in a secure and private conversation.

  • Groups and Communities

    Join groups and communities based on your interests or create your own. Connect with like-minded individuals, participate in discussions, and share content within these communities.

  • Privacy Controls

    Manage your privacy settings to control who can view your profile and posts. Customize your privacy preferences to ensure your content is shared with the right audience.

  • Content Moderation

    We prioritize creating a safe and inclusive environment. Our robust content moderation system helps detect and prevent the sharing of inappropriate or harmful content.
